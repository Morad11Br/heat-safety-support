Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Heat Safety collects location data only when you explicitly grant permission. This data is used solely to provide weather information for your area.

How We Use Your Information

Your location is used to fetch weather data from the National Weather Service. We do not sell, share, or store your location data on our servers.

Data Storage

All saved locations are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personal information to third parties.
